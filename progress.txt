[2026-03-04] ARCHITECTURE DECISION — Direct Wi-Fi Connectivity (v4.0)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DECISION: Tag connects directly to venue Wi-Fi. GSM and BLE gateway paths removed.
RATIONALE:
  The project is deployed as a venue-managed system. The venue IT team registers
  the device MAC address once on their network. The tag connects to venue Wi-Fi
  as a standard station and POSTs data directly to the cloud server over HTTPS.

  BLE gateway infrastructure (not required when direct Wi-Fi is available).
